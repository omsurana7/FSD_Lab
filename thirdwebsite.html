5 Historical Places in India

Taj Mahal, Agra

A magnificent white marble mausoleum built by Mughal emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal.

Red Fort, Delhi

A UNESCO World Heritage Site, this imposing fort served as the main residence of the Mughal emperors.

Qutub Minar, Delhi

The tallest minaret in India, it is a marvel of Indo-Islamic architecture and a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Hampi, Karnataka

The ruins of the Vijayanagara Empire, Hampi is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and a treasure trove of historical and architectural wonders.

Ajanta and Ellora Caves, Maharashtra

These cave temples, renowned for their intricate rock-cut sculptures and paintings, are UNESCO World Heritage Sites.
